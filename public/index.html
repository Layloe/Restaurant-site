History

WHO WE ARE. There’s nothing cookie-cutter about Jamie's Pizzeria. Not our pizzas. Not our people. And definitely not the way we live life. Around here, we don’t settle for anything less than food we’re proud to serve. And we don’t just clock in. Not when we can also become our best, make friends, and have fun while we’re at it. We’re the pizza company that lives life unboxed.

The Chef?  Mr. Villa himself. We’re not for people who want to blend in: pushing boundaries is part of our heritage. We have more than 3 restaurants and 35 team members. Whether it’s the original Stuffed Crust or putting a pizza in outer space, we never stop driving ourselves to deliver hot pizzas, fast every time – anywhere you want to enjoy it.

Started from the bottom now we're here.
